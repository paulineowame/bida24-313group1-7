Carrot Balls

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Flour
  • Cornstarch
  • Ketchup
  • Sugar
  • Black Vinegar
  • Water
  • Soy Sauce
  • Oil

Instructions

  1. Grate the carrots.
  2. Thinly slice the carrots.
  3. Steam carrots for 30 minutes themn brutally mash it.
  4. Add flour/cornstartch.
  5. Kneed dough until well and shape them into balls.
  6. Cook the balls in water.
  7. Mix ketchup, soy sauce, sugar, black vinegar, water and oil and cook till bubbling.
  8. Add carrot balls to Bubbling mixture and springle with spice of choice until well cooked
  9. Enjoy your meal!
