Mediterranean Chicken Salad with Creamy Tzatziki

Ingredients

    FOR THE CHICKEN

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Juice of one lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • FOR THE SALAD

  • 4 cups mixed greens(argula, spinach, or lettuce)
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cucumber sliced
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese

    • FOR THE CREAMY TZATZIKI SAUCE

  • 1/2 cup greek yoghurt
  • 1/2 cucumber, grated and squeezed to remove excess moisture
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
  • 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt the pepper to taste

Instructions

    FOR THE CHICKEN

  • Preheat a grill or Skillet to medium-high heat.
  • Season the chicken breasts with olive oil, dried oregano, garlic powder, salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
  • Grill or cook the chichen for 6-7 minutes per side, or until fully cooked and golden brown.
  • Remove from heat and let it rest for 5 minutes before slicing.

    • FOR THE SALAD

  • In a small bowl mix the greek yoghurt, grated cucumber, minced garlic, fresh dill, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  • Stir until well combined and set aside.

    • ASSEMBLY

  • In a large bowl, toss the mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber slices, kalamata olives, and red onion together.
  • Top the salad with the sliced grilled chicken and crumbled feta cheese.
  • Drizzle with the homemade tzatziki sauce and serve immediately
