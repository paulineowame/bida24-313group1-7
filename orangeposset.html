Orange Posset

Ingredients

  • 75g sugar (1/3 cup)
  • 5g orange zest (1/2 tbsp) (1 orange)
  • 200ml heavy cream (1 cup)
  • 30g orange juice (2 tbsp) (1/2 orange)
  • 10g lemon juice (1 tsp) (1/4 lemon)
  • 5g vanilla powder (1 tsp)
  • 2 orange shells (to serve)

Instructions

  1. Grate the zest of 1 orange.
  2. Mix lemon zest and Sugar.
  3. Add cream.
  4. Stir until it boils over medium heat.
  5. Remove the boiling mixture from the stove.
  6. Add orange and lemon juive and vanilla and mix.
  7. Strain the mixture thropugh a sieve and pour into the emptied oranges.
  8. Chill in the refregerator for at least 3 hours
  9. Ready to serve. Enjoy your meal.
