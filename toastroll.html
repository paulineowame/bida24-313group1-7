Toast Roll

Ingredients

  • Slices of bread
  • Shredded cheese
  • Ham strips
  • Butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Roll a biece of bread unti it is flat.
  2. Place the ham and cheese on the bread then roll it up and secure it with toothpicks(s).
  3. Dip in beaten eggs.
  4. You can put in the Airfryer at 180 degrees celcius for 10 minutes and flip in the middle, or, deep fry in hot oil and butter until golden brown.
  5. Serve warm and Enjoy!
